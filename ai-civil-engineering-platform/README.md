# AI-powered Civil Engineering and Data Analysis Platform

**Description:**

An advanced platform that leverages Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG), machine‑learning estimation, optimization algorithms, a conversational assistant, and intelligent analytics dashboards to support civil engineering projects and data analysis workflows.

## Key Features
- **RAG‑enabled knowledge base** for quick retrieval of engineering standards and past project data.
- **ML estimation models** for cost, material quantities, and timeline predictions.
- **Optimization engine** to propose design alternatives and resource allocations.
- **Conversational AI assistant** to answer domain‑specific queries and guide users.
- **Interactive analytics dashboard** with visualizations for project metrics, risk analysis, and performance tracking.

*This repository serves as a scaffold for building the full solution.*
